01 / The Pivot

From SERPs to scatter plots.

For nearly five years, I worked as a Senior SEO Executive at Techmagnate in New Delhi — reading algorithms, not to build them, but to reverse-engineer them. Every ranking factor was a hidden feature. Every traffic dip was an anomaly waiting to be explained.

Eventually the question changed. I stopped asking "why did this page rank" and started asking "can I model why." That question is what pulled me into data science — and into my current role as a Data Science Intern at Next Hikes Solutions in the Greater Delhi Area.

The SEO years didn't disappear. They became the domain expertise I now bring to messy, real-world datasets — the instinct for signal buried in noisy, high-cardinality data.
